Mexico City, Mexico

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is the capital of Spain's Catalonia. It has a rich history and beautiful old architecture designed by the famous Antoni Gaudi.

Chicago is home to the best pizza. It is also home to one of the world's best rated art museums, The Art Institute of Chicago.
